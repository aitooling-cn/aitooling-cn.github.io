1. ChatGPT: The All-Rounder

Strengths

ChatGPT remains the most versatile AI writing tool. With the GPT-4o model, it excels at technical writing, code documentation, and creative brainstorming. The GPT Store offers hundreds of specialized writing plugins.

Weaknesses

Chinese writing quality lags behind Claude. The free tier has significant limitations, and the "AI feel" can be noticeable in longer texts.

Best For

Technical bloggers, developers, users needing multimodal capabilities (text + image).

2. Claude: The Chinese Writing King

Strengths

Claude produces the most natural-sounding Chinese content. Its 200K context window handles entire books. The writing style is nuanced and rarely feels "AI-generated."

Weaknesses

Daily usage limits on the free tier. No image generation. Limited web search capabilities.

Best For

Chinese content creators, business writers, long-form content, academic writing.

3. Gemini: The Value Pick

Strengths

Massive 1M token context window. Deep integration with Google ecosystem. Native Google Search integration gives it an edge for research-heavy writing.

Weaknesses

Writing style tends to be conservative. Less creative than ChatGPT or Claude for storytelling.

Best For

Google Workspace users, research-heavy content, budget-conscious users.

🏆 Final Verdict

For Chinese Writing: Claude is the clear winner.

For Technical Content: ChatGPT takes the crown.

For Budget & Google Users: Gemini offers the best value.
